"Harnessing Technology For Sustainable Beekeeping Practices!"

Weight Sensor

Measures the weight of the hive to monitor honey production and detect any unusual activity.

Temperature Sensors

Monitors the internal temperature of the hive to ensure optimal conditions for bee health and productivity.

Sound Sensor

Analyzes the acoustic signals of the bees to detect swarming, queen events, and other behavioral changes.

Humidity Sensor

Tracks the moisture levels within the hive, preventing mold growth and ensuring a healthy environment.
